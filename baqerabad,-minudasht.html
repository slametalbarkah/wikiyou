Baqerabad, Minudasht

Baqerabad (Persian: باقراباد, also Romanized as Bāqerābād) is a village in Chehel Chay Rural District, in the Central District of Minudasht County, Golestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 74, in 21 families.
