Incheh Shahbaz

Incheh Shahbaz (Persian: اينچه شهباز, also Romanized as Īncheh Shāhbāz) is a village in Dowlatkhaneh Rural District, Bajgiran District, Quchan County, Razavi Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 110, in 28 families.
