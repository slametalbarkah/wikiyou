Larub, Landeh

Larub (Persian: لاروب, also Romanized as Lārūb; also known as Lārob) is a village in Olya Tayeb Rural District, in the Central District of Landeh County, Kohgiluyeh and Boyer-Ahmad Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 31, in 4 families.
