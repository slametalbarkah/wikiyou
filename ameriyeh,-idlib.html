Ameriyeh, Idlib

Ameriyeh, Idlib (Arabic: العامرية) is a Syrian village located in Hish Nahiyah in Maarrat al-Nu'man District, Idlib. According to the Syria Central Bureau of Statistics (CBS), Ameriyeh, Idlib had a population of 1027 in the 2004 census.
