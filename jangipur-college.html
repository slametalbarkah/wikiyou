Jangipur College

Jangipur College, established in 1950, is a college in Jangipur, Murshidabad, West Bengal, India. It offers under graduate and post graduate courses in arts, commerce and sciences. It is affiliated to University of Kalyani.

== Accreditation ==

Jangipur College was awarded B++ grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). The college is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC).
