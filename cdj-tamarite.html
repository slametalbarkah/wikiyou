CDJ Tamarite

Club Deportivo Juvenil Tamarite is a Spanish football team based in Tamarite de Litera, in the autonomous community of Aragon. Founded in 1960, it plays in Tercera Federación – Group 17, holding home matches at Estadio La Colomina.
