Keo Soksela

Keo Soksela (Khmer: កែវ សុខសិលា; born 1 August 1997) is a Cambodian professional footballer who plays as goalkeeper for Cambodian Premier League club Visakha and the Cambodia national team. His performance against rivals favourites Malaysia and Indonesia in major competitions being strongly attracted.





== International career ==

Soksela made his senior debut in a friendly match against Timor-Leste on 12 October 2018.
