Kelisay-e Sir

Kelisay-e Sir (Persian: کلیسای سیر‎, also Romanized as Kelīsāy-e Sīr; also known as Kelīsā-ye Gadik) is a village in Baranduz Rural District, in the Central District of Urmia County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 39, in 10 families.
