Caolan McColgan

Caolán (Irish pronunciation: [ˈkiːlˠaːnˠ, ˈkeː-]) is a masculine Irish given name, derived from the Irish word caol meaning "slender, narrow, fine". The name has a number of anglicisations, including "Kelan", "Keelan" and "Kealan".
