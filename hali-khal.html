Hali Khal

Hali Khal (Persian: هلي خال, also Romanized as Halī Khāl) is a village in Deraz Kola Rural District, Babol Kenar District, Babol County, Mazandaran Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 247, in 59 families.
